ABOUT US

Since 1993, Nordam Enterprises has been a trusted, family-owned business at the heart of Malawi’s power sector. We specialize in providing robust and reliable power solutions, from high-performance diesel generators and industrial engines to advanced solar power systems and energy storage. Our team of highly-qualified engineers, electricians, and mechanics brings decades of combined experience, ensuring expert service and support for every project.

Our commitment extends beyond just supplying quality products from leading brands like Perkins, Lister Petter, and FG Wilson. We offer a comprehensive suite of services including professional installation, ongoing maintenance, and 24/7 support to keep your operations running smoothly. We strive to build long-term relationships with our customers by providing exceptional, personalized service tailored to your unique needs.

We are proud to be the power solutions partner for a wide range of clients in Lilongwe and across Malawi, including the United States Embassy, German Embassy, British High Commission, European Union, and numerous banks, NGOs, schools, and hospitals. Our goal is to continue leading the power sector by delivering quality and reliability where it matters most.
